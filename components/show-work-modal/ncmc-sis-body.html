North Central Mindanao College (NCMC) - School Information System

PHP Laravel jQuery Vue.js Boostrap MySQL GoDaddy

NCMC is a leading research and community-based institution located in Maranding, Lanao del Norte, Philippines. It offers competent and outcome-based curriculum serving students from Early Childhood Education through College Education.

Fully developed from scratch, a School Management System using Laravel 5. The major modules included were Sections, Enrollments, Scholarships, Online Payments, POS, Invoices, Credit Memos and many more. Also consists Faculty Portal, and Student Portal.

I am the main developer assigned for this project from database structure (ERD) up to daily development and design.

Later on, we redevelop and used Laravel and Vue.js as its main tech stack.
